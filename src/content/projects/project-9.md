Dr. Pressure is a pressure washing company based in Nanaimo, British Columbia, serving communities across Vancouver Island. I worked remotely as a solo Website Designer and Developer from July 2025 to October 2025. The engagement concluded after I completed my deliverables and client communication ended.

At the outset, the project focused on enabling simple, non-technical content editing, delivering an SEO-friendly and responsive website, and supporting dynamic content such as testimonials and reviews. To achieve this, I selected the Stone theme for Astro in collaboration with the client and integrated Keystatic to provide a content editing experience that did not require access to the source code. I then configured the system and provided training so the client could manage the site independently.

However, due to limited client feedback and the absence of established brand assets, I defined the visual direction from scratch. As a result, I redesigned and organised visual assets to meet browser requirements, strengthened brand identity, and applied common UI design patterns, including a 90-10 colour rule for a clean, minimalist interface. In addition, typography was reviewed carefully to ensure legibility across devices.

Ultimately, the project delivered a functional and maintainable website, although client communication became limited during later stages, which prevented further iteration. While working with Astro and Keystatic was a valuable learning experience, it proved slower for client delivery. In future projects, I would prioritise stronger client engagement and clearer communication boundaries, and consider platforms such as WordPress to support faster content management workflows.
