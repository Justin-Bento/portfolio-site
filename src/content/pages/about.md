Justin Bento is a Technical Enthusiast based in Toronto that advocates for open-source technologies and builds digital literacy for individuals.

His work focuses on rejecting modern convenience in favor of system control, privacy, and community investment. 

Justin’s philosophy is centered on "learning, playing, and building things to a point where they are good enough, then leaving them alone."

He helps others navigate the "digital noise" by providing guidance on setting firm boundaries with technology, and educates people the importance of being intentional with technology.
